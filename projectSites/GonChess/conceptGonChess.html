Concept - Gon-Chess

× ↬ ↫

About the concept:
This "alternative chess game" is made to be played by 4 players simultaneously, either in a 2v2 or in a free-for-all.
The board consists of octagonal tiles and four independent 'player areas' connected with each other via a central 'shared area'. Inside the different areas you have to respect different movement rules to properly go along with the altered shape of the board.
The goal of Gon-Chess is the same as in the original version of chess: be the last player/team with a standing king.
But compared with the original game, each type of chess figure obeys to diverging movement rules adapted to the irregularly shaped board and tiles.
